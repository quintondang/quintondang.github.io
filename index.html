About

I spent a decade as a software engineer — owning design systems at Square and shipping experimentation tools at Optimizely. Throughout, I was drawn to the product side of the work: the why behind technical trade-offs, the alignment work that makes a roadmap real, and the cross-functional trust that lets teams actually move fast.

At Square, I drove product strategy for the Market Design System — leading the React 1.0 rollout, running an accessibility compliance initiative, and building the cross-team standards that kept a large, distributed engineering org coordinated.

I'm now focused on product management full-time: owning roadmaps and the stakeholder relationships that make them real, while bringing genuine technical fluency to every architecture and prioritization conversation.

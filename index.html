About

I'm a senior frontend engineer with 10 years of experience building design systems and accessibility-first UI at scale. My work centers on React and component architecture — shipping systems that get adopted org-wide and hold up under real usage by large engineering orgs.

At Square, I was a core contributor to Market, the company-wide React design system — building complex interactive components, leading a 1,000-issue accessibility remediation initiative, and driving the React 1.0 rollout across 100+ engineers and 16 product teams.

I'm drawn to frontend-leaning roles where the work touches product and design: teams that care about composability, accessibility, and building things that other engineers actually want to use.
